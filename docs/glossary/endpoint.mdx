<p className="cf-kicker">GLOSSARY · API</p>

An endpoint is a method plus route, such as `POST /auth/login` or `GET /me`.

Why it matters here: endpoint behavior defines what the frontend can rely on.

Where it appears: [API endpoints](/backend/identity/api-endpoints), [Auth flow](/backend/identity/auth-flow).

Related terms: [API](/glossary/api), [OpenAPI](/glossary/openapi).

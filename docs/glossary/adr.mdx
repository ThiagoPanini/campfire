<p className="cf-kicker">GLOSSARY · SPECS</p>

An ADR records a durable technical decision, its context, and its consequences.
Campfire keeps ADRs beside the spec that produced them.

Why it matters here: Spec 002 introduced accepted ADRs for PostgreSQL,
SQLAlchemy async, AWS deferral, UUID/time choices, LocalStack deferral, and the
backend Hexagonal + DDD pattern.

Where it appears: [ADR index](/sdd/adrs/index), [Spec 002](/sdd/specs/002-backend-auth-slice).

Related terms: [SDD](/glossary/sdd), [Spec-Kit](/glossary/spec-kit).

<p className="cf-kicker">GLOSSARY · SPECS</p>

Spec-Kit is the workflow used to produce `spec.md`, `plan.md`, `tasks.md`, and
supporting artifacts for a slice.

Why it matters here: Spec-Kit keeps Campfire's implementation slices small,
traceable, and constitution-bound.

Where it appears: [Spec-Kit methodology](/sdd/methodology/spec-kit), [Spec lifecycle](/sdd/methodology/spec-lifecycle).

Related terms: [SDD](/glossary/sdd), [Contract](/glossary/contract).

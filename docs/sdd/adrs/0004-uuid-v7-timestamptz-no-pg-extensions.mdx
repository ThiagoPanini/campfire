<p className="cf-kicker">ADR · ACCEPTED</p>

Generate UUIDs application-side, store time as UTC `timestamptz`, and avoid
Postgres extensions in the identity slice unless a future ADR proves support.

Related: [Data and persistence](/backend/identity/data-persistence), [Migration](/glossary/migration).
